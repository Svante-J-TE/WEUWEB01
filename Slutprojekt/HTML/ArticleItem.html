The best songs from the last decade

10. Drake, "Hotline Bling"

"You used to call me on my cell phone, Late night when you need my love" we've all heard it, we've all loved it. So it's to no suprise to find the song on this list.

9. Cardi B, “I Like It”

We like it too Cardi. This was the song of the summer in 2018 and even if it's overplayed, we just can't get enough of it.

8. Kacey Musgraves, “Follow Your Arrow”

With deep lyrics and a beautiful voice, Kacey took our hearts in 2013 with this song. A well deserved 8th spot.

7. Ariana Grande, “Thank U, Next”

Ariana never disappoints and with this song about exes she's grateful for she took the world by storm.

6. Kanye West, “Runaway”

With the release of Kanye West's 5th studio album "My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy", the single "Runaway" really ran away on the streaming charts. Good job Kanye.

5. Taylor Swift, “All Too Well”

This song from Taylor is actually about Jake Gyllenheaal and the months they spent together. I don't mind some gossip, as long as it's something positive. Taylor you rock.

4. Beyoncé, “Formation”

"Formation" is a strong song from a strong woman. Beyoncé has earlier stated that the song is the importance of owning one's identity. A important message and a great song, perfect for the top 10.

3. Adele, “Rolling in the Deep”

Another girl's song about a ex-boyfriend, i'm starting to see a trend, not that i'm complaining.

2. Kendrick Lamar, “Alright”

The Compton rapper Kendrick Lamar has had one of the best 10 years in the music industry. This song was the begining of everything.

1. Robyn, “Dancing on My Own”

In this tale of heartache Robyn is watching the man she loves dancing with another woman, while he is unaware of her presence on the dance floor. A great message and a great song. A perfect song well deserved of the number 1 spot.
